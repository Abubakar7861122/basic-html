-------------------- Cricket League --------------------

Cricket Premier League

We are a prosperous club with a long tradition of nurturing talented and aspiring young cricketers to be the best that they can be. Reading Cricket Club is a friendly, vibrant and progressive cricket club that features competitive and successful teams in coaching campuses.
The Club runs three Saturday League sides competing in the Home Counties Premier League and City Cricket League, a Sunday XI, and a thriving and successful junior section for both boys and girls and a Ladies team which is becoming a real force in ladies cricket locally.
We are very proud of what AbuBakar Cricket Club has achieved over the last few years, including buying our own ground, building a new pavilion and supporting the local community by playing high quality and enjoyable cricket at all levels. We are a very friendly club, and welcome new players and supporters alike.
Our grounds are one of the loveliest places to play and watch cricket,while at the same time enjoy a fun and friendly atmosphere in which to play sport and socialise.
